# First Frame Test - Rooftop + Avion

## Reference image

Use:

```text
photos/01_frontal_serios.jpg
```

## Goal

Generate the first 5-6 seconds of the ad:

- Calin on a futuristic rooftop.
- Modern European city at dusk.
- Large silent sci-fi aircraft passing far behind him.
- Premium business tech look.
- Minimal motion to preserve face identity.
- No lip-sync.
- No text generated by AI.

## Runway / Kling prompt

```text
Use the reference photo to keep the same man identity, face shape, hairstyle and clothing. Vertical 9:16 cinematic tech commercial. The man stands confidently on a futuristic rooftop above a modern European city at dusk. A large silent sci-fi aircraft passes far behind him. Subtle holographic business workflow panels float around him. Natural slow camera push-in, realistic lighting, premium business technology advertisement, calm serious confident expression, minimal body movement, no speaking, closed mouth, no readable text, no logos, no extra people, 6 seconds.
```

## Negative prompt

```text
blurry face, distorted face, distorted hands, extra fingers, extra people, readable text, random letters, logos, cartoon, anime, low quality, extreme facial changes, open mouth, lip sync, smiling too much, unsafe stunt, falling, explosions, chaotic background
```

## Recommended settings

- Mode: image-to-video.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16.
- Duration: 5 or 6 seconds.
- Motion: low / subtle.
- Camera movement: slow push-in.
- Creativity: medium-low if available.
- Face/identity preservation: enabled if available.
- Text generation: disabled if available.

## Save output as

```text
ai_clips/01_rooftop_avion_test_v1.mp4
```

## Accept if

- Face still looks recognizably like Calin.
- No weird mouth movement.
- No broken hands in the main visible area.
- Aircraft is in the background, not touching or threatening the subject.
- The shot feels premium, not cartoon/fantasy.
- There is no generated text or fake logo.

## Reject/regenerate if

- Face changes too much.
- Mouth tries to talk.
- Hands become the visual focus and look broken.
- Aircraft looks dangerous or explosive.
- Scene looks like gaming art instead of a business tech ad.
